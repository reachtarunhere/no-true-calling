About The Podcast

Casually candid conversations with curiously clever, confident creatures continually changing themselves as they passionately pursue pretty creative colourful callings - personally, professionally, perpetually!

This podcast on reinvention and multipotentiality celebrates renaissance humans such as the OG - Leonardo Da Vinci.

Hosted by two really rakish rationals residing in a sweepingly successful, small, sunny sojourn - Singapore.
